Company of Ash — Calculator guide
RANGED FIRE TAB

Build your attacking squad using up to 3 unit slots. Each slot has its own faction, unit type, weapon, model count, and range toggle — set Optimum (+1 Fortune), Normal, or Long (−1) per weapon based on the distance you measured at the table. Weapon range bands are shown in the slot info line.

Set the target and its cover state. Cover 1 is light cover (−1 Fortune). Cover 2 is heavy cover or scouts in any cover (−2 Fortune). If the target is infantry and the defending player chooses to Go to Ground, select it before rolling — the first attack resolves at the current cover level, all subsequent attacks treat the unit as Cover 1 minimum. Go to Ground adds 1 suppression to the target.

ERA appears on ERA-equipped vehicles. When a hit is scored the calculator rolls automatically — 4+ negates the hit and all crits. ERA is one use only; uncheck the box after it fires.

TOUGH units: infantry-class weapons can still affect Tough units but only if the Fortune die rolled a natural 6 or higher. The calculator flags this in the log.

What this tool does not handle (resolve manually): MaDDs intercept (5+ on D6, roll before using calculator), Guided weapon rerolls (your choice at the table), Power shields (5+ save per hit after calculator resolves), ERA expiry tracking across turns.

MELEE TAB

Enter stats directly — skill die, models, attacks per model, weapon type, suppression, and keywords. No unit database lookup needed. Works for any unit including wolves and mercenaries not yet in the ranged database.

Suppression removes models from the attack pool (suppressed models still take casualties). 2+ suppression = −1 Fortune on all melee rolls. 4 = Pinned (all models suppressed from attacking, still valid casualties).

APPD (Cloud Runner close defense vs charging infantry): flip attacker and defender, set Overwatch mode, enter D4 skill / 1 attack per model / 3 models / standard weapon type. Resolve as a ranged attack in the Ranged tab before committing to the melee tab.

CRITICAL HITS

Critical results display automatically when vehicles take damage. Check off triggered effects on your printed army sheet. Sensor damage stacks — multiple hits can render a vehicle combat-ineffective. Catastrophic = vehicle destroyed immediately.
